# Evalgist

Evalgist builds AI-assisted evaluation tools for document-heavy professional decisions.

The product is designed for people who evaluate documents a few times a year, not for a living: selection committees, hiring managers, academic teams, school boards, and other groups that need a defensible decision without turning evaluation into a full-time workflow.

## Product Principle

Every AI-assisted judgment must be tied to cited evidence from the source material. Evalgist is built around reviewable outputs, literal quotes, and human decision-making. The system helps surface fit, risk, consistency, and supporting evidence; the human remains the decider.

## Current Products

- **[Evalgist Shortlist](https://evalgist.ai/shortlist)** — AI-assisted resume screening with quoted evidence. Live.
- **Evalgist Grade** — consistent grading support for open-ended exam answers. In development.
